About the page

Before we started this assignment we sat down as a group and made a short sketch of what we wanted the website to look like. We tried to decide what semantic tags we wanted to include in our website. Even though everyone on our group is fairly new to using HTML, we tried our best to include as many as possible. We started with a header where we added our fictional characters name plus a menu bar. We then added a body to our website, where we wrote most of the content. Here we included paragraphs and pictures. We decided to make our website this way because it seemed the most simple way to structure our website, even though our code might seem a little messy.
